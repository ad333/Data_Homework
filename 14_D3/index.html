Poverty vs Healthcare

I analyzed relationship between poverty level and the lack of health insurance in US by state. There was a moderately strong correlation (coerr = 0.52) between these two, suggesting that people living in poverty do not get proper access to healthcare.

Among all states, Mississippi and New Mexico had the highest level of poverty whereas Texas had the highest percentage of people with no healthcare coverage.
